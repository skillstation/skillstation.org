## CBSE Bridge Course (Maths) – 45 Days  
**For IIT-JEE Aspirants**

### Course Introduction
This 45-day CBSE Bridge Course is designed to smoothly transition students from
CBSE-level Mathematics to IIT-JEE expectations. The course prioritizes high-impact
topics such as Vectors, Three-Dimensional Geometry, and Calculus, followed by
core algebraic concepts and probability. Emphasis is placed on concept clarity,
application-based problem solving, and exam-oriented practice.
