About Skill Station Academy

Skill Station Academy is a student-focused learning platform dedicated to bridging the gap between university-level concepts and national-level competitive exams such as GATE, IIT-JEE, and other technical entrance exams.

Founded by IIT Madras alumni, Skill Station Academy is a non-profit education, research, and skill training institution and functions as a science and mathematics institute. The academy also offers industry-relevant training programs aimed at enhancing employability and technical competence.

Our programs emphasize conceptual clarity, exam-oriented problem solving, and efficient revision strategies, guided by experienced mentors.

Course Overview

This Engineering Mathematics Crash Course for GATE 2026 is a 15-day intensive revision program designed for final-year engineering students who have already applied for GATE 2026.

Eligibility

  • 4th-year Engineering students (all branches)
  • Applicants of GATE 2026
  • Students from all colleges across India

Mode of Delivery

  • Online: Live classes via Zoom
  • Offline: For students of the hosting university

Hosting university will be confirmed on or before 10 January 2026.

Expected Hosting Institutions:

  • Anurag University, Ghatkesar, Hyderabad
  • VIT, Vellore
  • SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur, Chennai

Fee Structure

Course Duration

12 January 2026 – 30 February 2026

Syllabus Coverage

  • Calculus: Limits, continuity, differentiability, MVT, integration
  • Vector Calculus: Gradient, divergence, curl, integrals
  • Complex Variables: Analytic functions, CR equations, series
  • Differential Equations: ODEs and PDEs
  • Probability & Statistics: Distributions, correlation, regression
  • Numerical Methods: Linear/non-linear equations, integration
