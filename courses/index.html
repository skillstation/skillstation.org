About Us

Skill Station Academy is a student-focused learning platform dedicated to bridging the gap between university-level concepts and national-level competitive exams such as GATE, IIT-JEE, and other technical entrance exams.

Founded by IIT Madras alumni, Skill Station Academy is a non-profit education, research, and skill training institution functioning as a science and mathematics institute. We also offer industry-relevant training programs to enhance employability and technical competence.

Our programs emphasize conceptual clarity, exam-oriented problem solving, and efficient revision strategies, guided by experienced mentors.

Foundation Mathematics (Class X / XI / XII)

Foundation Mathematics lectures are based on the NCERT syllabus but extend beyond CBSE board standards, primarily intended for IIT-JEE aspirants.

These courses are also ideal for students aiming to score 90% and above in board examinations, as they significantly strengthen conceptual understanding.
