About TiSAT-2025

The TiSAT (Talent in Science and Technology) 2025 is an initiative by Skill Station Academy aimed at identifying and nurturing young scientists and technologists. Through this program, students gain access to mentorship, resources, and a platform to showcase their innovative ideas. Additionally, eligible candidates will have the opportunity to receive the prestigious Young Scientist Fellowship.

Young Scientist Fellowship Program

The Young Scientist Fellowship Program is designed to support aspiring scientists with mentorship, funding, and resources to explore innovative projects. Selected fellows will work with leading scientists and academicians to further their research and development goals.
