About the Course

This course is crafted to help students master the concepts of CBSE Class 12 Mathematics, with a special focus on those preparing for IITJEE Mains and Advanced. Additionally, the topics covered will enhance CBSE board exam preparation and provide students with a competitive edge over their peers.

  • Live Interactive Online Sessions – Engage with expert faculty in real-time.
  • Self-Paced Learning – Access course lectures, worksheets, and exams anytime via our web portal and mobile app for 1 year.
